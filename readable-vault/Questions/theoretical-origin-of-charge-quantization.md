Empirically, [[charge-quantization]] is well established. Dirac showed that the mere *existence* of a magnetic monopole would force charge quantization via gauge invariance. But magnetic monopoles have never been observed. Are there other theoretical derivations — e.g., from anomaly cancellation in the Standard Model — that explain quantization without invoking monopoles? This question is touched on but not resolved in [[purcellelectricity-and-magnetism-3rd-edition]].
